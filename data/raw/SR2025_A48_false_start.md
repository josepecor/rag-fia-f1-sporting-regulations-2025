## 48.1

Any of the penalties under Articles 54.3a), 54.3b), or 54.3c) will be imposed on any driver who is judged to have:

### a)

Moved after the four (4) second light is illuminated and before the start signal is given by extinguishing all red lights, as defined in Article 44.10 b), or;

### b)

Positioned his car on the starting grid in such a way that the transponder is unable to detect the moment at which the car first moved from its grid position after the start signal is given, or;

### c)

Any part of the contact patch of its front tyres outside of the lines (front and sides) at the time of the Start signal.
