# Article 50 – Starting procedure suspended

## 50.1 If, after one or more formation laps behind the safety car, track conditions are considered unsuitable to start the sprint session or the race, the message “START PROCEDURE SUSPENDED” will be sent to all Competitors using the official messaging system and all cars must enter the pit lane behind the safety car. The procedures described in Articles 57 and 58 must then be followed. The race will start when the safety car leaves the pit lane as described in Article 58.8.
